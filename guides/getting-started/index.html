Getting Started

Deploy your first app in under a minute

This guide will walk you through deploying your first app with itsalive.co. By the end, you'll have a live website with a public URL.

Prerequisites

You'll need:

  • Node.js installed (for running npx)
  • A folder with your web files (HTML, CSS, JS)
  • An email address for verification

Step 1: Create Your App

If you don't have a project yet, create a simple one:

mkdir my-app
cd my-app

Create an index.html file:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My First App</title>
</head>
<body>
  <h1>Hello, World!</h1>
  <p>My app is alive!</p>
</body>
</html>

Step 2: Deploy

In your project folder, run:

npx itsalive-co

You'll be prompted for:

  • Subdomain: Pick a unique name (e.g., "my-awesome-app")
  • Email: Your email for verification

Step 3: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to confirm your ownership.

The CLI will detect your verification automatically and proceed to upload your files.

Step 4: You're Live!

Once the upload completes, you'll see your URL:

âœ¨ https://my-awesome-app.itsalive.co

That's it! Your app is now live on the internet.

Updating Your App

Made changes? Just run npx itsalive-co again in the same folder. It will remember your subdomain and deploy the updated files.

Next Steps
