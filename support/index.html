Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I deploy my first app?

Run npx itsalive-co in your project folder. Pick a subdomain, verify your email, and you're live. The whole process takes about 10 seconds.

What files get deployed?

Everything in your current directory (except node_modules, .git, and other common ignore patterns). Your index.html becomes your homepage, and all other files are available at their relative paths.

How do I add authentication?

Call our login API from your frontend. We handle the magic link emails, verification, and session management. Check out the Authentication Guide for details.

Is there a free tier?

Yes! The free tier includes up to 10 sites, auth, database, hosting, 100 emails per day, and 10,000 visitors per month. Pay-as-you-go for AI API usage.

Can I use my own domain?

Custom domains are available on the Pro plan ($50/year per site). Point your domain's DNS to us and add it in your dashboard.
