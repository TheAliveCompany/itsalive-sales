Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers so you can get back to building

How do I deploy?

Run npx itsalive-co in your project folder. Pick a subdomain, verify your email. You're live in about 10 seconds.

What files get deployed?

Everything in your current directory (except node_modules, .git, and common ignore patterns). Your index.html becomes your homepage. No build step required.

How do I add auth?

One API call. We handle magic link emails, verification, and sessions. No Auth0, no Clerk, no OAuth config. See the Authentication Guide.

What's free?

Up to 10 sites with auth, database, and hosting. 100 emails/day, 10k visitors/month. Enough to validate your idea before committing anything.
