AI Credits & Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing for AI-powered features

How Credits Work

Credits are the universal currency for AI features on itsalive.co. Every AI request consumes credits based on the model used and token count. New accounts start with 50,000 free credits to get you started.

Simple Math

1 credit = approximately 1 token. Most short conversations use 500-2,000 credits. A typical chatbot interaction costs about $0.001-0.01.
